# Claude for Small Business — What It Actually Is

**Skill level: Foundations**

A free plugin (toggle) inside Claude Cowork, launched May 13, 2026 — not a new pricing tier. It ships 15 ready-to-run workflows and 15 skills, with connectors to QuickBooks, PayPal, HubSpot, Canva, Docusign, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365.

**Worth knowing before you install it:** the plugin itself is free, but it runs on top of an existing paid Claude plan (Pro, Max, or Team) — several sources note this is a common point of confusion since launch. "Free plugin" does not mean "free Claude."
